# Sales Report Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview

The Sales Report Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze business sales performance for May 2026. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, quantity sold, customer segments, product performance, and regional trends, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance and profitability.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Analyze regional sales trends and customer segments.
* Track key business metrics using KPI cards and visualizations.
* Support strategic business decisions through actionable insights.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
* **Power Query**
* **Data Modeling**
* **Data Visualization**

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Sales, Profit, and Quantity KPI Cards
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Product Performance Tracking
* Customer Segment Analysis
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* Trend Analysis Visualizations
* Business Performance Insights

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Identified top-selling and most profitable products.
* Analyzed sales distribution across different regions.
* Evaluated performance by customer segment.
* Highlighted revenue and profit trends to support business growth.

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains sales transaction data including:

* Order Details
* Product Information
* Customer Segments
* Regional Data
* Sales, Profit, and Quantity Metrics

## 🚀 Outcome

Developed a user-friendly and interactive dashboard that transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights, helping stakeholders monitor performance and make informed decisions.
